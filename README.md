# Formula E — Auto-Updating Calendar (Evergreen)

Subscribe in Apple/Google/Outlook Calendar:

```
https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Creative-fw/formula-e-calendar/main/FormulaE.ics
```

- Every session of the ABB FIA Formula E World Championship — Practice, Qualifying, Race
- **Evergreen:** auto-detects the current season from the official Formula E API and
  rolls over to the next season automatically — no yearly maintenance
- The nine qualifying micro-sessions (groups + duels) are merged into one
  "Qualifying" block, matching the broadcast
- Times in UTC — your calendar converts to local time automatically
- Races flagged 🏁 with a 1-hour alert
- Synced daily at 04:00 UTC from `api.formula-e.pulselive.com` (official data,
  explicit per-session GMT offsets)

Part of the same feed family as
[FIA WEC 2026](https://github.com/Creative-fw/wec-2026-calendar),
[ELMS 2026](https://github.com/Creative-fw/elms-2026-calendar),
[MLMC 2026](https://github.com/Creative-fw/mlmc-2026-calendar) and
[F1 2026](https://github.com/Creative-fw/f1-2026-calendar).
